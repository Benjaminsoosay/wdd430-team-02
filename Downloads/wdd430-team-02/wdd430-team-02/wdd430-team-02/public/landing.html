About Handcrafted Haven

A full-stack artisan marketplace built with modern web technologies

Handcrafted Haven is a full-stack web application that connects artisans and crafters with customers who value unique, handcrafted items. The platform serves as a virtual marketplace where sellers can showcase their products, share their stories, and manage listings.

Buyers can browse, filter, review, and purchase handmade goods. The project emphasises community, sustainable consumption, and support for local artisans.

🎯 Project Purpose

  • ✓ Connect artisans with customers worldwide
  • ✓ Showcase unique handcrafted products
  • ✓ Support local artisans and sustainable consumption
  • ✓ Build a community around handmade goods

User Stories (MVP)

12 core features for our minimum viable product

#1 - As a new user, I want to create an account so I can save my profile and favorites. High Priority
#2 - As a returning user, I want to log in securely to access my dashboard. High Priority
#3 - As a seller, I want to add a new product with title, description, price, and images. High Priority
#4 - As a seller, I want to modify an existing product's details. Medium Priority
#5 - As a seller, I want to remove a product I no longer offer. Medium Priority
#6 - As a user, I want to see all products ordered by date or popularity. High Priority
#7 - As a seller, I want to mark an item as sold. Medium Priority
#8 - As a user, I want to filter products by category, price range, or availability. Low Priority
#9 - As a mobile user, I want the interface to work perfectly on any screen size. High Priority
#10 - As a user, I want to switch between dark and light themes. Low Priority
#11 - As a user, I want to search products by keyword. Medium Priority
#12 - As a user, I want my cart and preferences to persist after page refresh. High Priority
