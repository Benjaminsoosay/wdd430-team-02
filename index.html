✨ Handcrafted Haven – Project Vision

A vibrant marketplace connecting artisans & conscious buyers. Unique handmade items, seller stories, community ratings.

Color Palette

Primary #3B82F6 · Secondary #10B981 · Warm accent

Typography

Inter (sans-serif)

Headings: bold · Body: regular · Accessible scale

Layout & Components

Responsive grid (cards), sticky header, max-w-7xl centered, soft shadows, hover effects.

📋 User Stories & Work Items

12 core features for Handcrafted Haven (sprint backlog) · Prioritised for MVP

1. User Registration

High

As a new buyer or artisan, I want to create an account so I can save my profile and start selling/buying.

2. User Login

High

As a returning user, I want to log in securely to access my dashboard, cart, and saved items.

3. Create Product Listing

High

As a seller, I want to add a new product with title, description, price, and images to showcase my craft.

4. Edit Product

Medium

As a seller, I want to modify an existing product's details (price, description, images).

5. Delete Product

Medium

As a seller, I want to remove a product that is no longer available.

6. Browse & View All Products

High

As a user, I want to see all products sorted by date or popularity to discover new items.

7. Ratings & Reviews

Medium

As a buyer, I want to leave a rating and written review for a product I purchased.

8. Filter by Category / Price

Low

As a user, I want to filter products by category, price range, or material to find what I love.

9. Responsive Layout (Mobile)

High

As a mobile user, I want the interface to work seamlessly on any device (phone, tablet, desktop).

10. Dark/Light Mode Toggle

Low

As a user, I want to switch between dark and light themes for comfortable browsing.

11. Search Products

Medium

As a user, I want to search for products by keyword (title, artisan name, description).

12. Persistent Shopping Cart

High

As a user, I want my cart items to remain after page refresh (localStorage or database).
